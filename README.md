# Power BI Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance and support data-driven decision-making.

## Dashboard Features
- Executive Dashboard
- Tactical Dashboard
- Operational Dashboard
- Interactive slicers
- KPI tracking
- Revenue analysis
- Customer insights
- Lead conversion analysis

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Lead Conversion Rate
- Customer Retention
- Sales Growth

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Performance
- Revenue trends were monitored over time to identify seasonal fluctuations and support better sales planning.
- High-performing periods were identified to help optimize marketing and operational strategies.

### Customer Insights
- Customer segmentation enabled the business to understand purchasing behavior across different customer groups.
- Repeat customers contributed significantly to overall sales, highlighting opportunities to strengthen customer retention initiatives.

### Sales Performance
- Individual sales representative performance was analyzed to identify top performers and areas requiring additional support.
- Lead conversion metrics helped evaluate the effectiveness of the sales pipeline.

### Service Performance
- Service requests were analyzed by category and location to identify high-demand services.
- Geographic analysis highlighted cities and regions generating the highest business volume, supporting expansion and resource allocation decisions.

### Operational Insights
- Contract status tracking provided better visibility into active, completed, and pending service agreements.
- Delivery and service timelines helped monitor operational efficiency and identify opportunities for process improvement.

### Dashboard Value
- The dashboard consolidated multiple business datasets into a single interactive reporting solution.
- Interactive filters and drill-down capabilities enabled management to analyze performance by city, state, service type, salesperson, and time period.
- Real-time KPI monitoring reduced manual reporting effort and supported faster data-driven decision-making.

### Business Impact

- Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to centralize sales and operational reporting.
- Improved visibility into key business metrics through automated KPI tracking.
- Enabled management to monitor sales trends, customer behavior, and regional performance using interactive visualizations.
- Reduced manual reporting by providing a centralized and easy-to-use dashboard for business analysis.
